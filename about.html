ADORE CONSTRUCTIONS

Adore construction (AC) has been established in 2020 A.D. (2077 B.S.) with the prime objective of providing the quality services in the field of Civil and Electrical Engineering. OUR VISION

With the thought of “We build, you adore” our vision is to build the construction that people adores and admire. With the consideration of our cultural and the economical values our vision is to become the one of the best construction company of the nation and lead the nation towards the better future.

OUR MISSION

Our goal is to provide the best professional services to our clients in a timely and promised manner, build an effective system with progressive development and strong work ethics, continuously innovate our products and services, and respect and represent ourselves in the local community to create a harmonious work environment.

OUR OBJECTIVE

We work constantly by adopting and implementing modern techniques and technology, maintaining standards within the company and community, creating a human-centered culture to increase skills and dedication, working in diverse construction sectors, and respecting moral values to produce collective effort for a better future.

What We Do



Instrumentation and monitoring

We design structures to meet both requirements and sustainability, with a focus on both aesthetics and long-lasting strength. We also provide guidance to clients on executing the design work through engineering ideas and innovative approaches.

Design and Formulation

AC provides services and systems to monitor the safety and sustainability of various aspects of construction, such as roads, buildings, and drainage systems. We help coordinate with different levels of people, including engineers and operators, to identify and resolve risks, improve work methods and designs, and perform regular compliance work.

Supervision of work

We only do work that creates value and can provide the best solution and guidance for the construction of your dream within your budget, including performing a valuation of the work or property to help with economic decisions.
